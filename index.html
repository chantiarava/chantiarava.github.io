About Me

I am pursuing my postgraduate studies in Applied Geography and Geoinformatics at the Central University of Karnataka. My academic and research interests include Remote Sensing, GIS, and Geospatial Analysis. I specialize in Spatial Analysis, OBIA, AHP, Hotspot Analysis, Change Detection, Hydrology, and Watershed Analysis, applying these methods to analyze spatial patterns and geospatial relationships for real-world decision making.
