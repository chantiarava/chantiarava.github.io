About

I am currently pursuing an integrated M.Sc. in Applied Geography and Geoinformatics at the Central University of Karnataka. My research focuses on remote sensing, GIS-based environmental modelling, and climate-related spatial analysis.

My recent work involves fire-modified soil erosion modelling using the RUSLE framework and satellite datasets in Google Earth Engine. I am particularly interested in geospatial applications for natural hazard assessment, urban climate studies, and land system analysis.
