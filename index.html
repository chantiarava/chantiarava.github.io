About Me

Research Intern at IIIT Hyderabad specializing in fire-modified soil erosion modelling using the RUSLE framework and satellite data. Currently pursuing an integrated M.Sc. in Applied Geography and Geoinformatics at the Central University of Karnataka. My work focuses on Google Earth Engine (GEE), Remote Sensing, and GIS for analysing landscape changes and environmental processes. My expertise includes OBIA, AHP, and change detection, applying advanced spatial analysis for climate and natural hazard studies.
