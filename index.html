About

I have completed my integrated M.Sc. in Applied Geography and Geoinformatics from the Central University of Karnataka. I am currently working as a Research Assistant at IIIT Hyderabad, focusing on geospatial modelling and environmental analysis.

My research involves fire-modified soil erosion modelling using the RUSLE framework and satellite datasets in Google Earth Engine. My interests include remote sensing, GIS-based environmental modelling, and applications in natural hazards, land use change, and climate studies.
